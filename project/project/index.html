About ITS and Renting Equipment

ITS is a department of OIS that maintains, installs, and troubleshoots intructional technology and equipment in classrooms and offices for ATU. This portal is designed to allow students and faculty to rent equipent such as microphones, video camuras, projectors from ITS for a short time.

In the Inventory tab, you will find a list of all the equipment currently available for rent from ITS along with some information about the make and model.

In the Cart tab, you will find any items you have selected for rent from the inventory tab.

In the Checkout tab, you will be able to complete the rental form so that ITS will be able to contact you about returning the equipment if over the selected rental period.

Renting Process

  • Select Equipment from Inventory
  • Enter Cart
  • Register or sign in to save your information
  • Checkout and pick up your equipment from ITS office in RPL 328
