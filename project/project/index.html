About ITS and Loaning Equipment

ITS is a department of OIS that maintains, installs, and troubleshoots intructional technology and equipment in classrooms and offices for ATU. This portal is designed to allow students and faculty to borrow equipment such as microphones, video cameras, projectors from ITS for a short time.

In the Inventory tab, you will find a list of all the equipment currently available for loan from ITS along with some information about the make and model.

In the Checkout tab, you will be able to complete the loaner form so that ITS can contact you about returning the equipment if over the selected rental period.

Loaning Process

  • Select Equipment from Inventory
  • Check item availability and choose your item from the dropdown menu.
  • Checkout and pick up your equipment from ITS office in RPL 328
